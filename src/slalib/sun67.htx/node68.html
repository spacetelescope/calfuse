SLA_DRANGE - Put Angle into Range

ACTION:
Normalize an angle into the range (double precision).
CALL:
D = sla_DRANGE (ANGLE)

GIVEN:

ANGLE D angle in radians

RETURNED:

sla_DRANGE D ANGLE expressed in the range .
