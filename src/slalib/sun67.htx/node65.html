SLA_DPAV - Position-Angle Between Two Directions   

ACTION:
Returns the bearing (position angle) of one celestial direction with respect to another (double precision).

CALL:
D = sla_DPAV (V1, V2)

GIVEN:

V1 D(3) direction cosines of one point
V2 D(3) directions cosines of the other point

RETURNED:

sla_DPAV D position-angle of 2nd point with respect to 1st

NOTES:
1.
The coordinate frames correspond to , etc..
2.
The result is the bearing (position angle), in radians, of point V2 as seen from point V1. It is in the range . The sense is such that if V2 is a small distance due east of V1 the result is about . Zero is returned if the two points are coincident.
3.
The routine sla_DBEAR performs an equivalent function except that the points are specified in the form of spherical coordinates.
