SLA_DTT - TT minus UTC   

ACTION:
Compute TT, the increment to be applied to Coordinated Universal Time UTC to give Terrestrial Time TT.

CALL:
D = sla_DTT (DJU)

GIVEN:

DJU D UTC date as a modified JD (JD-2400000.5)

RETURNED:

sla_DTT D TT-UTC in seconds

NOTES:
1.
The UTC is specified to be a date rather than a time to indicate that care needs to be taken not to specify an instant which lies within a leap second. Though in most cases UTC can include the fractional part, correct behaviour on the day of a leap second can be guaranteed only up to the end of the second .
2.
Pre 1972 January 1 a fixed value of 10 + ET-TAI is returned.
3.
TT is one interpretation of the defunct timescale Ephemeris Time, ET.
4.
See also the routine sla_DT, which roughly estimates ET-UT for historical epochs.
