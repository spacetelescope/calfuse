SLA_PVOBS - Observatory Position & Velocity   

ACTION:
Position and velocity of an observing station.
CALL:
CALL sla_PVOBS (P, H, STL, PV)

GIVEN:

P D latitude (geodetic, radians)
H D height above reference spheroid (geodetic, metres)
STL D local apparent sidereal time (radians)

RETURNED:

PV D(6) (AU, AU s-1, true equator and equinox of date)

NOTE:
IAU 1976 constants are used.
