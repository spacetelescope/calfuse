SLA_EPB2D - Besselian Epoch to MJD   

ACTION:
Conversion of Besselian Epoch to Modified Julian Date.
CALL:
D = sla_EPB2D (EPB)

GIVEN:

EPB D Besselian Epoch

RETURNED:

sla_EPB2D D Modified Julian Date (JD-2400000.5)

REFERENCE:
Lieske, J.H., 1979. Astr.Astrophys. 73, 282.
