SLA_EQECL - J2000 to Ecliptic

ACTION:
Transformation from J2000.0 equatorial coordinates to ecliptic longitude and latitude.

CALL:
CALL sla_EQECL (DR, DD, DATE, DL, DB)

GIVEN:

DR,DD D J2000.0 mean (radians)
DATE D TDB (formerly ET) as Modified Julian Date (JD-2400000.5)

RETURNED:

DL,DB D ecliptic longitude and latitude (mean of date, IAU 1980 theory, radians)
