SLA_DCMPF - Interpret Linear Fit   

ACTION:
Decompose an linear fit into its constituent parameters: zero points, scales, nonperpendicularity and orientation.

CALL:
CALL sla_DCMPF (COEFFS,XZ,YZ,XS,YS,PERP,ORIENT)

GIVEN:

COEFFS D(6) transformation coefficients (see note)

RETURNED:

XZ D x zero point
YZ D y zero point
XS D x scale
YS D y scale
PERP D nonperpendicularity (radians)
ORIENT D orientation (radians)

NOTES:
1.
The model relates two sets of coordinates as follows. Naming the six elements of COEFFS a,b,c,d,e & f, the model transforms coordinates into coordinates as follows:
x2 = a + bx1 + cy1
y2 = d + ex1 + fy1
