SLA_EPJ2D - Julian Epoch to MJD   

ACTION:
Convert Julian Epoch to Modified Julian Date.
CALL:
D = sla_EPJ2D (EPJ)

GIVEN:

EPJ D Julian Epoch

RETURNED:

sla_EPJ2D D Modified Julian Date (JD-2400000.5)

REFERENCE:
Lieske, J.H., 1979. Astr.Astrophys., 73, 282.
