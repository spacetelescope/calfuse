SLA_DTP2S - Tangent Plane to Spherical   

ACTION:
Transform tangent plane coordinates into spherical coordinates (double precision)

CALL:
CALL sla_DTP2S (XI, ETA, RAZ, DECZ, RA, DEC)

GIVEN:

XI,ETA D tangent plane rectangular coordinates (radians)
RAZ,DECZ D spherical coordinates of tangent point (radians)

RETURNED:

RA,DEC D spherical coordinates (radians)

NOTES:
1.
The projection is called the gnomonic projection; the Cartesian coordinates are called standard coordinates. The latter are in units of the distance from the tangent plane to the projection point, i.e. radians near the origin.
2.
When working in rather than spherical coordinates, the equivalent Cartesian routine sla_DTP2V is available.
