SLA_PREBN - Precession Matrix (FK4)   

ACTION:
Generate the matrix of precession between two epochs, using the old, pre IAU 1976, Bessel-Newcomb model, in Andoyer's formulation.

CALL:
CALL sla_PREBN (BEP0, BEP1, RMATP)

GIVEN:

BEP0 D beginning Besselian epoch
BEP1 D ending Besselian epoch

RETURNED:

RMATP D(3,3) precession matrix

NOTE:
The matrix is in the sense:
v1 = Mv0
where v1 is the star vector relative to the mean equator and equinox of epoch BEP1, M is the matrix RMATP and v0 is the star vector relative to the mean equator and equinox of epoch BEP0.

REFERENCE:
Smith et al., 1989. Astr.J. 97, 269.
